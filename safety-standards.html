KONEKT Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Our Commitment: KONEKT is committed to providing a safe environment for all users and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content.

Prohibited Content

KONEKT strictly prohibits:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Grooming behaviors or communications
  • Any content that exploits or endangers children
  • Sharing or soliciting inappropriate content involving minors

Age Requirements

  • Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account
  • Users under 18 are considered minors and receive additional protections
  • We may request age verification when necessary

Detection and Prevention

We employ multiple measures to detect and prevent CSAE:

  • Content moderation systems to identify prohibited material
  • User reporting tools for flagging inappropriate content
  • Review of reported content by trained moderators
  • Proactive monitoring of high-risk content and behaviors

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns through:

  • In-App Reporting: Tap the report button on any content or profile
  • Email: willsonowusu90@gmail.com

All reports are reviewed promptly and treated with the highest priority.

Response Procedures

When CSAE content is identified:

  • Content is immediately removed from the platform
  • The account is suspended pending investigation
  • Reports are filed with relevant authorities including NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children)
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

Compliance with Laws

KONEKT complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • Reporting requirements to NCMEC and other designated authorities
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies
  • Retention of evidence as required by law
  • Regional and national child protection regulations
